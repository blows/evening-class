Text: 

Artist and designer Thomas Pausz came to Evening Class to talk about his teaching experience at the Iceland Academy of Arts in Reykjavik and to present his current research project at the Delfina Foundation in London. Some participants of Evening Class will collaborate with him to design a documentation of his research on community gardens, soil, filters and dent-de-lion.
